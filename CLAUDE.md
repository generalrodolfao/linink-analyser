# CLAUDE.md

## Product Context

LinkedIn Pro-Copilot is an AI-native micro SaaS focused on:
- LinkedIn profile optimization
- AI personal branding
- intent-based outreach

The product must feel premium and minimalist.

## Engineering Philosophy

- Build modular systems
- Avoid overengineering
- Keep the MVP lean
- Prefer readability over cleverness
- Prefer maintainability over shortcuts

## UI Philosophy

Inspired by:
- Linear
- Vercel
- Notion

Rules:
- dark mode first
- large spacing
- subtle animations
- avoid visual clutter

## Important Constraints

NEVER:
- automate LinkedIn actions
- store LinkedIn credentials
- use browser automation

## AI Rules

All AI outputs should:
- sound natural
- avoid spam tone
- avoid generic marketing language
- prioritize clarity and specificity
